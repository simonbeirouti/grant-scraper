# Funding for QLD manufacturers looking to increase energy efficiency measures
  
## Program
Manufacturing Energy Efficiency Grant Program QLD

## Summary
The Manufacturing Energy Efficiency Grant Program QLD offers matched funding between $7,500 and $50,000 to eligible Queensland manufacturing SMEs for energy efficiency projects. Key benefits include reducing energy costs and emissions. Eligibility requires being a GST-registered, ABN-holding SME with 5â€“200 FTE employees, operating in QLD for at least 12 months.
