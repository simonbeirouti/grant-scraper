# Sponsorship for heritage event and activity organisers
  
## Program
Heritage Sponsorship Program WA

## Summary
The Heritage Sponsorship Program WA offers funding and partnerships to heritage organisations for events or activities aligning with specific strategic outcomes, such as sustainability, education, community engagement, and tourism. Applications are open until June 30, 2025.

## Application Complexity
**Medium**

## Key Tip
Applicants should clearly map each activity to the Heritage Council's strategic outcomes, providing specific examples of how their event contributes to these goals.
